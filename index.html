Moscow - is a city contrasts and possibilites
The capital of Rassia, where 12.7 million people live - is a mix of eras, styles and destinies. Here, the golden domes of temples are adjacent to the skyscrapers of Moscow-City and the quiet alleys of Arbat are adjacent to the bustling metropolis, where 9.5 million passengers take the subway every day.
Moscow - is a city of opportunities: 40+ leading universities, financial and technological hub, cultural mosaic. But it's also a city to live in, with everything: a green oasis, night lights, and cozy courtyards.
